Documentation for this format

This structure is derived from Extension

Summary

Complex Extension: This basis extension describes information identifying norwegian real property.

  • cadastralUnitNumber: integer: Identification of a real property in Norway is defined using gårdsnummer, bruksnummer and kommunenummer. In addition real property can be rented out, the area of the real property that is rented is measured and is further identified by its festenummer. In addition some real property is split into sections identified by its seksjonsnummer. Gårdsnummer is called Cadastral unit number in English.
  • propertyUnitNumber: integer: Identification of a real property in Norway is defined using gårdsnummer, bruksnummer and kommunenummer. In addition real property can be rented out, the area of the real property that is rented is measured and is further identified by its festenummer. In addition some real property is split into sections identified by its seksjonsnummer. Bruksnummer is called Property unit number in English.
  • leaseholdNumber: integer: Identification of a real property in Norway is defined using gårdsnummer, bruksnummer and kommunenummer. In addition real property can be rented out, the area of the real property that is rented is measured and is further identified by its festenummer. Festenummer is called Leasehold number in english.
  • condominiumUnitNumber: integer: Identification of a real property in Norway is defined using gårdsnummer, bruksnummer and kommunenummer. In addition some real property is split into sections identified by its seksjonsnummer. Seksjonsnummer is called Condominium unit number in English.
  • municipality: Coding: The norwegian kommune identifies an administrative geographic unit in Norway called kommune by a predefined number and its official name.
